How It Works ?

It is as simple as a 3 step process.

1. Open the app

Download the app by clicking here or head over to the download section to download it, then install and launch the app.

2. Create an account

Click the signup button and create an account but before you use it you have to verify your account. Check your email ibox for the verification email.

3. Login

You have to now login with the email id and password you entered when signing up.

Ask it anything.

Search for the capital of vietnam or find translations in over 100 languages. Ask Susi for your flight status, and what the weather’s like when you get there.

Susi has special feature that is auto improvement. The more you interact with it the more better it gets.
